### Textbooks

1. **Error Control Coding**, S. Lin and D. Costello - 2nd Edition, Prentice-Hall, 2004.
2. **The Theory of Error-Correcting Codes**, F. MacWilliams and N. Sloane - North-Holland Publishing Company, 1977.
3. **Fundamentals of Error-Correcting Codes**, W. Huffman and V. Pless - Cambridge University Press, 2003.
4. **Introduction to Coding Theory**, R. M. Roth, Cambridge University Press, 2006.
5. **Introduction to Coding Theory**, J.H. van Lint, Springer-Verlag (Graduate Texts in Mathematics, (3rd edition)), 1999.
6. **Fundamentals of Convolutional Coding**, R. Johanneson and K.Sh. Zigangirov, IEEE Press, 1999.
