### Textbooks

**1. Error Control Coding**<br/>
BY: S. Lin and D. Costello - 2nd Edition, Prentice-Hall, 2004<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of BCH codes, Reed-Solomon codes, finite field theory, and decoding algorithms including Berlekamp's algorithm._

**2. Introduction to Finite Fields and Their Applications**<br/>
BY: R. Lidl and H. Niederreiter - Cambridge University Press, 1986<br/>
_Fundamental concepts of finite field theory, minimal polynomials, and primitive elements essential for understanding BCH codes._

**3. Algebraic Codes for Data Transmission**<br/>
BY: Richard E. Blahut - Cambridge University Press, 2003<br/>
_Detailed treatment of cyclic codes, BCH codes, and algebraic decoding techniques with practical implementation aspects._

**4. The Theory of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: F. J. MacWilliams and N. J. A. Sloane - North-Holland, 1977<br/>
_Classic reference on algebraic coding theory covering BCH codes, weight distributions, and bounds on code parameters._

**5. Fundamentals of Error-Correcting Codes**<br/>
BY: W. Huffman and V. Pless - Cambridge University Press, 2003<br/>
_Accessible introduction to BCH codes, cyclic codes, and their decoding procedures with numerous examples._
